New Marine Beach Rooms is respected by our customers

Welcome to New Marine Beach Rooms, your cozy and affordable escape nestled near the beautiful Pondicherry Beach and the historic charm of White Town. At New Marine Beach Rooms, we pride ourselves on offering a comfortable stay at budget-friendly prices, making it the perfect choice for travelers looking for quality accommodation without breaking the bank. Whether you're here to explore the sun-kissed shores, the quaint French architecture, or simply relax and unwind, our guest house provides a convenient and welcoming base for your adventures.
